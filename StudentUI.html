User Guide & Terms and Conditions

Welcome to the Golden Success College Cebu Online Library. Please read and accept the terms before proceeding.

User Guide:

  • Look for books in the categories.
  • Search for books using the search bar.
  • Borrow available books and track your borrowed items.

Terms & Conditions:

1. Deposit all things outside. Bring in only sheets of paper and pens for notes.

2. Observe silence. Idle conversations, loud laughter, loitering, eating, group discussions, and all acts of vandalism are strictly prohibited.

3. In order not to annoy other library users, doing activities such as software demo, sewing projects, and industrial activity works are not allowed in the library.

4. Keep things in proper order. Push your chair under the table and return books & periodicals to their proper places.

5. Handle books and other materials with care. They are intended to serve many users after you.

6. Books are intended to be used at the reading area only. It must not be taken out of the library without proper knowledge by the school librarian.

7. Library users are subject to voluntary inspection before leaving the library.

8. Reserved Books may be borrowed one hour at a time or from 11:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. or overnight from 5:00 p.m. to 8:00 a.m. the following day.

9. Fiction books may be borrowed for one week, renewable for another week unless there is a prior demand for these books.

10.Other books may be borrowed for three (3) days, renewable for another three (3) days unless there is a prior demand for these books by another user.

11.A student may borrow a maximum of two (2) books at a time.

Library Fines and Penalties:

For overdue books, the following fines and charges apply:

Fiction books – Php 5.00 a day, including Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays.

Reserved books – Php 10.00 for the first day and Php 20.00 for the succeeding days, including Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays.

A lost book must be reported immediately to avoid accumulation of fines.

A book lost must be paid for or replaced with the same title and a ten percent processing fee. If the book is not available in the market, the borrower must pay the price set by the school plus a ten percent processing fee.

Damaged books beyond reasonable wear must be replaced with another of the same title of the latest edition or paid for at the same price.

Students who fail to observe library regulations and procedures will be referred to the principal for disciplinary action.
